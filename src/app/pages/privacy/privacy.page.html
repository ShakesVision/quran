Privacy Policy

Quran Hifz Helper • February 2026

Overview

Quran Hifz Helper is a free, open-source Progressive Web App (PWA) for reading and memorizing the Holy Quran. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

What We Collect

Short Answer: We collect nothing.

Quran Hifz Helper is designed to operate entirely on your device. No personal data is sent to our servers. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device using your browser's local storage:

  • Reading Progress — Your last page/surah/juz viewed
  • Bookmarks — Personal bookmarks and notes you create
  • Memorization Progress — Hifz tracker data (juz/surah completion)
  • Settings — Font size, theme preferences, tajweed colors
  • Notes — Personal notes you add while reading

This data is never sent anywhere. It exists only in your browser's local storage on your device.

What We Don't Do

  • ❌ We don't track your reading habits
  • ❌ We don't store your data on servers
  • ❌ We don't use analytics or telemetry
  • ❌ We don't show you ads
  • ❌ We don't sell or share your data
  • ❌ We don't require account creation
  • ❌ We don't use cookies for tracking

External Resources

The app loads Quranic text and audio from these trusted sources:

  • archive.org — Quran text and scanned mushaf images
  • quran.com — Translations, word-by-word data, tafseer
  • everyayah.com — Audio recitation files
  • tafsir.app — Detailed tafseer commentary (external link)

When you access these external resources, their own privacy policies apply. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies directly.

Service Worker & Offline Caching

To enable offline functionality, the app uses a Service Worker to cache:

  • App shell (UI components and styles)
  • Quran text files you have accessed

This cache is stored locally on your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

Your Data Control

You have complete control over all your data:

  • View: Open your browser's developer tools → Application → Local Storage
  • Backup: Use the app's export feature to download your data as JSON
  • Delete: Clear your browser's local storage or uninstall the app

HTTPS & Security

The app is served over HTTPS from https://quran.shakeeb.in. Your connection is encrypted, preventing unauthorized interception of data in transit.

Third-Party Services

The app links to or uses these external services:

  • OneSignal: Push notification library (optional, can be disabled)
  • Google Play Store: If installed as Android app
  • Telegram: Feedback and support link

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them if you use these integrations.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for all ages, including children. Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no privacy risk to children using the app.

Data Retention

All your data is retained on your device until you manually delete it. Clearing your browser cache or uninstalling the app will remove all data permanently.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. The latest version is always available at https://quran.shakeeb.in/privacy.
